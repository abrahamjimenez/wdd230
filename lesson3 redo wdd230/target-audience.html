Target Audience

  • Residents that want to socialize, interact, and have a fun time.
  • Tourists that want to know what activities are occurring.
  • Families, groups of friends.

Scenarios

  • What are some activities in Tri-Cities, Washington?
  • Where can I find activities in Tri-Cities?
  • What events are happening next week near me?
  • What programs are available in Tri-Cities?
  • What resources are available in the Tri-Cities?

Personas

  • A family member that is wanting for their family to do outdoor activities with a group of people
  • A group of kids who are wanting to go to outdoor, social activities for fun.
